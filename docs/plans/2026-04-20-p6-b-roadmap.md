## Review notes

- `ModeState` already exists from the earlier `modes.zig` translation and
  should be reused instead of retranslated in `P6.B`.
- `setMode` remains a later bridge concern because its behavior crosses mode
  storage, cursor state, margins, and screen switching.
- `P6.B1` is intentionally chosen as the smallest independent slice that
  unlocks later bridge work without inventing placeholder cursor or page
  structures.
